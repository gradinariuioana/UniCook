Italian Pasta

Welcome! Let's start cooking!

Today we're cooking pasta!

Ingredients:

  • 250g. uncooked penne pasta
  • 300g. chicken breast
  • 100g. cheese
  • 1 cup halved cherry or grape tomatoes
  • 50g. butter
  • 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions and then drain.
  2. Rinse the pasta with cold water. Beware! The pot is very hot!
  3. In a nonstick wok, stir-fry the chicken in butter until chicken is no longer pink.
  4. Add the mushrooms, tomatoes and seasoning
  5. Then add the pasta and heat through for another 5 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat. Sprinkle with cheese; let stand until melted.

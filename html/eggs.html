Pouched Eggs with Avocado

Welcome! Let's start cooking!

Today we're cooking a very delicious, very easy-to-make healthy breakfast!

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small ripe avocado
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 2 tomatoes, halved
  • 2 slices seeded wholemeal bread

Instructions:

  1. Heat a non-stick frying pan, add a little oil, then cook the tomatoes, cut-side down, in the pan until they have softened.
  2. Meanwhile, heat a pan of water, carefully break in the eggs and leave to poach for 1-2 mins until the whites are firm but the yolks are still runny.
  3. While the eggs are cooking, toast the bread and smash the avocado on each piece of toast.
  4. Lastly, place the poached eggs on top of the toast. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, salt, pepper or fresh herbs.

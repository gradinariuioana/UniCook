Chicken Breast Pie

Welcome! Let's start cooking!

Today we're cooking a very delicios appetizer suited for any occasion.


Ingredients:

  • 300g. chicken breast
  • 50g. red pepper
  • 100g. cheese
  • 50g. flour
  • 150ml. milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of pepper

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the meat: Boil the chicken breast and then mince it
  2. Mix the dry ingredients: Whisk the flour and baking powder in a large bowl. Set aside.
  3. Mix the wet ingredients: Whisk the eggs, milk and salt until combined.
  4. Fold the dry ingredients into the wet: Carefully add the dry ingredients to the wet ones and mix with a wooden spoon or rubber spatula until just combined.
  5. Fold in the mix-ins: All that's left to do is adding the chicken and the cheese.
  6. Prepare the pan and oven: Arrange a rack in the middle of the oven and heat to 220oC.
  7. Fill the pan and bake: Pour the composition into the pan. Bake at 200oC for about 25 minutes.

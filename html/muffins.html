Moist Muffins

Welcome! Let's start cooking!

Today we're cooking moist muffins, a very delicious desert that can be not only enjoyed alone but also combined with a glass of milk or orange juice.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 110g. melted butter
  • 110g. sugar
  • 140g. flour
  • 1 pouch of Vanilla Sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • a few drops of rum essence
  • chopped chocolate

Instructions:

  1. Mix the dry ingredients: Whisk the flour and baking powder in a large bowl. Set aside.
  2. Mix the wet ingredients: Whisk the eggs, sugar and butter until combined.
  3. Fold the dry ingredients into the wet: Carefully add the dry ingredients to the wet ones and mix with a wooden spoon or rubber spatula until just combined.
  4. Fold in the mix-ins: All that's left to do is adding the chocolate and the essence.
  5. Prepare the pan and oven: Arrange a rack in the middle of the oven and heat to 220oC. Line a standard 12-well muffin pan with papers liners
  6. Fill the pan and bake: Divide the batter amongst the prepared muffin wells, about 1/3 cup of batter per well. Bake at 180oC until the muffins are golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean, about 30 minutes.
